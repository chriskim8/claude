Cancel Rules

Automatically suppress clinic sessions when specified conditions are met. Canceled sessions appear on the calendar and can be reinstated.

Rule Trigger Cancels Applies to Status
Post-call clinic cancel Night Call + following day All clinic sessions All residents Active
ICU rotation cancel ICU block rotation All clinic sessions All residents Active
Simple cancel rule — the 80% case. Covers call-heavy programs: when a resident is on Night Call or the following day (post-call), their clinic sessions are automatically suppressed. Classic equivalent: "Cancel Clinic" checkboxes in schedule Preferences. ~140 manual deletions/month eliminated at a 20-resident Q3 program.

Advanced cancel rule — rotation-based trigger. Covers programs where certain rotations preclude all clinic (e.g., ICU, inpatient night float). Trigger is the block rotation assignment rather than a call assignment. The coordinator can also target specific training levels — e.g., PGY-1s lose clinic on ICU; PGY-3 attendings keep theirs. Classic equivalent: Rule Writer advanced cancel clinic rules.

Reinstatements are logged for ACGME compliance. Reinstated sessions show green ✓ — distinct from never-canceled sessions. Cancel rules suppress; delete is permanent. A manually reinstated session remains active even if the rule still applies, until the override is explicitly removed.
